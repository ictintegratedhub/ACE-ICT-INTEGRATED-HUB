Your Trusted One-Stop Tech Hub

ICT Integrated Hub is your trusted one-stop tech hub in Nigeria, proudly serving clients for over 3 years. We are dedicated to empowering students, professionals, businesses, and organizations with reliable, efficient, and affordable ICT solutions.

We have assisted over 1,000 students with educational insights, exam registrations (JAMB, Post-UTME, WAEC/NECO GCE, NOUN), and vital resources to help them succeed. Our consultancy services — including systems setup, network topology, and career guidance — have helped scale numerous businesses and build strong professional identities for both companies and individuals.

Additionally, we specialize in seamless identity services, providing NIN slips, National ID cards, BVN printouts, and national identity verifications across different states. Our branding expertise covers eye-catching e-flyers, web development, company/project branding, and data analysis to elevate your digital presence.

“At ICT Integrated Hub, we combine expertise with a customer-first approach to make technology accessible and stress-free. Whether you’re a student chasing academic goals, a business aiming to grow, or an individual needing secure identity solutions, our committed team delivers excellence every time.”
