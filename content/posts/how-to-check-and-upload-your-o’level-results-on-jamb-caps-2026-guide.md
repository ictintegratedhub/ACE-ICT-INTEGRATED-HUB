# How to Check and Upload Your O’Level Results on JAMB CAPS (2026 Guide)

Did you know that scoring over 250 in your UTME does not guarantee university admission if your O’Level results are missing from the JAMB portal?

Every single year, thousands of qualified Nigerian students lose their hard-earned admission slots simply because their WAEC, NECO, or NABTEB results were not properly uploaded to the **Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) Central Admissions Processing System (CAPS)**.

If you registered for your exams as "Awaiting Result" (AR), or if you want to verify that your data is perfectly synchronized before Post-UTME screening begins, this step-by-step guide will protect your admission profile from costly technical errors.

## Why You Must Verify Your O’Level Upload Immediately

The admission landscape has evolved. Universities are strictly matching institution portal data with the centralized JAMB CAPS network.

> **Critical Compliance Note:** JAMB will not entertain external corrections for discrepancies in candidate data once the institutional screening window shuts down. If your portal reads _“Awaiting Result”_ when an institution compiles its shortlists, the system automatically skips your profile.

Checking your status takes less than five minutes, and it gives you complete peace of mind as schools open their 2026/2027 screening application portals.

## Step-by-Step: How to Check If Your O’Level Result is on JAMB CAPS

You can perform this initial check directly on a mobile smartphone or a personal computer. Follow these steps carefully:

### Step 1: Access the Facility Portal

Open your preferred mobile or desktop web browser and navigate directly to the official portal at [efacility.jamb.gov.ng](https://efacility.jamb.gov.ng/).

### Step 2: Switch to Desktop Mode (Crucial for Mobile Users)

If you are accessing the portal on a smartphone, tap the three vertical dots in the top right corner of your browser (Chrome or Safari) and select **"Desktop Site"** or **"Request Desktop Website."**

_Why this matters:_ The standard mobile view often hides the side menu navigation links required to open the CAPS dashboard.

### Step 3: Log In to Your Profile

Type in the exact email address and password you used during your initial registration cycle. Tap the login button to open your dashboard.

### Step 4: Locate the CAPS Link

Scroll down the dashboard landing page to locate the purple or blue button labeled **"Check Admission Status."** Select your current examination year and input your registration number, then click **"Access my CAPS."**

### Step 5: Verify the O’Level Tab

On the left-hand navigation panel, tap on **"My O'Level."** Your grades should load on the center screen immediately.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Can I upload my O'Level results by myself using my phone?

No. While you can easily _view_ your status from a mobile phone, the actual uploading action must be performed via an authorized registration system using official merchant access parameters. Attempting to force an upload via standard consumer browsers will fail.

### Can I combine WAEC and NECO results on JAMB CAPS?

Yes. If you sat for two different sittings to clear your required 5 core credits (including Mathematics and English Language), both result sheets can be securely processed and linked to your individual admission file.
